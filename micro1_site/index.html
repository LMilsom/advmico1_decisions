Welcome to Advanced Micro 1: Economics of Decision Making. In this course, we will rigorously cover the foundations of decision theory. My policies:

  1. Before lectures, you must complete the "MWG" and "papers" readings.
  2. Before exercise classes, you must complete the problem sets.
  3. Lectures will be recorded and uploaded the day after. Classes will not be recorded.
  4. Office hours are by appointment; email me :)
  5. Assessment is 100% closed-book exam joint with the other half of the course.
