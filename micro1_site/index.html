Advanced Microeconomics 1: Economic Decision Making

Mondays, 16:00–18:00, HOGM.00.0085· Thursdays, 11:00–13:00, KOL.00.0004 · Luke Heath Milsom luke.heathmilsom@kuleuven.be

Six weeks on the foundations of choice, demand, production and risk, following Mas-Colell, Whinston & Green, Microeconomic Theory (Chapters 1–3, 5 and 6). Eight lectures, one computational practical, three exercise classes.

Every block of theory in this course is the engine of a live empirical literature, and the lectures treat it that way. The problem sets do not: they stay technical, because the prelim will be. Slides and sheets are posted below as we go — sheets a week ahead of their class, and you are expected to have attempted them.
